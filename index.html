Food for a family

Food is a basic necessity for survival and also the key to a healthy life. Without regular healthy meals children struggle to go to school, carers struggle to work and communities are left battling to survive.

Shelter for a family

Our properties include shared houses, self-contained units and two and three bedroom villas, and are staffed by employees who understand that every person is an individual with unique goals and support needs.

Education for a child

Early childhood is a critical time for cognitive, social, and physical development. Unfortunately, in developing countries, many children under five fail to reach their developmental potential.
